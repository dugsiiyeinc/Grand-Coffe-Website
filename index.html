Services

Our café provides a variety of freshly made coffee drinks, including Mocha, espresso, cappuccino, and latte. Customers can also enjoy delicious pastries and snacks in a comfortable and relaxing environment. We focus on quality coffee and friendly service to give every guest a great experience. ☕

Mocha coffe

Mocha coffee is a rich and delicious drink that combines espresso, chocolate, and milk to create a smooth and sweet coffee flavor. 🍫

Hot Chocolate

Hot chocolate is a warm and sweet drink made with milk and melted chocolate or cocoa powder, perfect for relaxing and enjoying a rich chocolate flavor. ☕

Tea Original

Original tea is a natural and refreshing drink made by brewing tea leaves in hot water, offering a simple and classic taste enjoyed around the world. 🍃
